Civilization V Creation Tool

Leader

Any Civilization needs a strong willed Leader to lead them into prosperity. In terms of Civilization V, the Leader.xml page is used to configure and develop the artificial intelligence of your civilization, determining how the AI will generally respond to trade, development, and war. Before any of that though, lets start with your leader's reference name. This is so your Civilization will know what Leader is thiers. The format for leader reference names in Civilization V is; LEADER_MY_LEADER.


Now its time for art defines. These are set up by a separate XML file in order to organize the Leader Scene, and other elements. Wu Zeitan's Art Defines for example are located in Wu_Scene.xml.


As for their icon, the process is similar to all other xml files. Simply input the .dds file the icon is in and input its index.



Basic Attributes

Now lets sort out some general guidelines for the leader. Each one is fairly self explanatory, such as how loyal is a Leader after they made an agreement? How chatty are they? How much do they hate acts of warmongering? Each of these are on a simple scale, 1 to 10. The higher the value, the more inclined your leader is towards certain behaviors.















Major Civilization Bias

Now it is time for your Leader's biases towards Major Civilizations. These attributes will determine how your Leader will see other Civilizations in game.








Minor Civilization Bias

These values will represent how your Leader reacts to minor civilizations, also known as City States.






Civilization Flavors

These values will represent how your Leader deals with internal policies, such as unit production, city growth, and desire for base yields, such as science, culture, et cetera.







































Trait

Now lets set up your leader's trait. Every Civilization in Civilization V has a Trait, and the Leader acts as an intermediary between the Civilization and the Trait. Traits are written in the form TRAIT_MY_TRAIT.


Lore

Finally, lets write your leader's lore. You will only enter their name here. We will not be generating a civilopedia entry for your leader. If you want to write a civilopedia entry, please check a Leader.xml page.
